Polyaspartic/Polyurea vs. Epoxy Coatings

Unlike epoxy, polyaspartic and polyurea coatings are ideal for New England’s freeze-thaw cycles. They offer fast cure times, flexibility, and superior UV resistance—making them the top choice for garage floor coatings in Brookfield and beyond.

What is Polyaspartic/Polyurea Coating?

Polyurea provides flexibility and strength, while polyaspartic forms a UV-stable topcoat. Together, they create a seamless, non-porous concrete coating that resists stains, moisture, abrasion, and hot tire pickup.

Originally developed for industrial use, this system is perfect for garages, basements, patios, and commercial floors throughout Central Massachusetts.
